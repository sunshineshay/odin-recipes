Broccoli Chedar Soup

Description

Few things are better in life than eating some warm soup on a cold night. This is a delicious soup that you can easily prepare in 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4 Tablespoons Butter
  • 1/2 Medium Onion, Chopped
  • 2-3 Cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 4 Tablespoons All Purpose Flour
  • 2 Cups Low Sodium Chicken or Vegetable Stock
  • 1 tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1/4 tsp Paprika
  • 3 Cups Broccoli Florets
  • 1 Large Carrot, Grated or Finely Chopped
  • 2 Cups Half & Half
  • 8 Ounces of Grated Cheddar Cheese

Steps to Prepare

  1. Melt butter in a large dutch overn or pot over medium-high heat. Add the onion and cook 3-4 minutes or until softened and light gold. Add the garlic and saute for another minute.
  2. Add flour and whish for 1-2 minutes or until the flour begins to turn golden in color. Pour in the chicken stock, broccoli florets, carrots, and seasoning. Bring to a boil then reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 15 minutes or until the broccoli and carrots are cookied through.
  3. Stir in half & half and cheddar cheese and simmer for another minute. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  4. Serve with toasted crusty bread or in a bread bowl if desired.
