Chicken Alfredo

Description

Who doesn't love a plate of delicious Chicken Alfredo? According to Wikipedia, Fettucine Alfredo is a pasta dish of Italian origin. It consists of: Fresh Fettuccine that is tossed with a mixture of Parmesan Cheese and butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp. Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • 2 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • Kosher Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • 1 1/2 Cup of Whole Milk
  • 1 1/2 Cup of Low-Sodium Chicken Broth
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic, Minced
  • 8 Ounces of Fettuccine
  • 1/2 Cup of Heavy Cream
  • 1 Cup of Freshly Grated Parmesan
  • Freshly Chopped Parsley, for garnish

Steps to Prepare:

  1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat the oil. Add the chicken and season with salt and pepper. Cook until golden and cooked thorugh, about 8 minuts per side. Let rest 10 minutes, and then slice.
  2. Add milk, broth, and garlic to the skillet. Season with salt and pepper and bring to a simmer. Add fettuccine, stirring frequently for about 3 minutes. Let cook until al dente, about 8 minutes more.
  3. Stir in the heavy cream and Parmesan until combined. Simmer until sauce thickens.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the sliced chicken. Garnish with parsley.
