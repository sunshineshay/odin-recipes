Sandwich

Description

When you think of something to eat for lunch, you may think of a sandwich. Sandwiches are one of the easiest foods to prepare and they can be greatly customized to appeal to anyone.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Your Choice of Sandwich Fillings (Meat, Cheese, Peanut Butter, Jelly, Vegetables, etc.)
  • Condiments (if needed)

Steps to Prepare:

  1. Place your bread on a plate and spread your condiments (if needed) on the bread.
  2. Layer your sandwich fillings on the bread and close the sandwich together.
  3. If you are feeling fancy, you can cut your sandwich diagonally.
